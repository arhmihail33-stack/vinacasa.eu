# RAIDAVA Blog Hero Images

Hero images for blog posts, separated by source:

## svg/
Auto-generated SVG hero cards produced by Razvanis during the pre-launch period
(before June 2026 relocation). Each card uses deterministic selection based on
post slug + domain, rendering one of 4 templates (BANDĂ, COLȚ, CHENAR, LITERA MARE)
in one of 3 color variants.

Visual vocabulary: Muntenia vernacular palette (cream, dark oak, brick red, straw),
țesătură diamond patterns, traditional book-frame motifs.

Legal and regulatory posts (`romanian_regulations` domain) use SVG hero cards
**permanently by design**. Staged documentary photography of paperwork is not
authentic to RAIDAVA.

## real/
Documentary photography from the actual RAIDAVA farm, added incrementally
post-June 2026. Selection is deterministic by hash within each domain folder.
See `real/README.md` for naming convention and selection rules.
